Help the Avengers collect more Infinity Stones than Thanos!
Rules:
1) You can only move the Infinity Stones on your side
2) Each time you move, you pick up all the Infinity Stones on a planet and distribute them in a counter clockwise direction to the next planet
3) If the last Infinity Stone of a move landed on your mancala (sides of the board), then you can move again
4) If the last Infinity Stone of a move landed on an empty planet on your side and there are stones in the opposite side planet, then the stones in the two planets will be captured in your mancala
